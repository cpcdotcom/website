Who We Are

At CleverPath Consulting, we are passionate about technology and its potential to transform businesses. We specialize in providing end-to-end tech solutions that help our clients streamline their operations, enhance their online presence, and drive growth. We believe that every business is unique, and we strive to tailor our services to meet the specific requirements of each client. We offer a wide range of tech solutions, including website creation, app development, e-commerce solutions, cloud hosting, and more. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the industry to ensure that we can deliver innovative and cutting-edge solutions that help our clients stay ahead of the competition.

Partha Mallik

Co-founder

Partha is a highly accomplished professional with over 17 years of experience working for esteemed multinational corporations such as Walmart Labs and Oracle, as well as startups. With a strong background in software development, Partha brings extensive technical expertise to the table while also demonstrating a deep understanding of business requirements. He firmly believes in striking the perfect balance between theoretical solutions and practical implementation for optimal results. This pragmatic approach allows him to effectively address challenges and deliver real-world solutions that align with organizational goals. Beyond his professional endeavors, Partha has a diverse range of interests. He finds solace in long distance running, trekking, and indulging in a game of badminton. These activities not only contribute to his personal well-being but also foster a balanced mindset that positively influences his work.

Anirudh Prakash

Co-founder

Anirudh is a seasoned software engineer with a passion for creating innovative applications. With over a decade of industry experience, he has honed his skills in building robust software solutions from scratch and guiding engineering teams towards success. Throughout his career, he has played a role in the startup ecosystem, witnessing the birth and acquisition of multiple companies. His experience in these dynamic environments has instilled in him a knack for navigating the ever-evolving tech landscape. Anirudh excels in developing cloud applications, harnessing the power of scalable infrastructure and cutting-edge technologies to craft seamless user experiences.  He is continuously expanding his knowledge and embracing emerging tools and frameworks. His commitment to professional growth ensures that he stays ahead of the curve and provide clients with cutting-edge solutions.
